Bridging Creativity with Technical Expertise

I'm a full-stack software engineer from SoCal with a distinctive background. Before delving into the realm of code, I was acknowledged on national platforms as a poet. While seemingly worlds apart, both coding and poetry demand precision, a knack for patterns, and a bit of creativity.

My approach to software engineering emphasizes the art behind the science. I believe in clean code, not just because it's efficient, but because there's a certain elegance to it. It's this blend of aesthetics and functionality that I bring to projects, especially when venturing into areas like AI, Data Analysis, and Bioinformatics.

Beyond the screen, you might find me at the beach, immersing myself in a new game, or hiking alongside my hound pup, Orphy. Regardless of the setting, my commitment remains: to craft solutions that are as imaginative as they are technical.
